iSync and SonyEricsson Symbian/UIQ 3.0 finally speaking

Tuesday, 14 Aug 2007     apple ⋅ personal ⋅ macosx ⋅ sw ⋅ it ⋅ symbian ⋅ curiosity ⋅ english ⋅ sony ⋅ ericsson ⋅ projects ⋅ utility

Thanks God, now I can [en:iSync]!!!

As someone probably remember, I switched from an [en:Nokia E70] to a [en:Sony Ericsson W950i].

In the beginning, it was not possible to synchronize my [en:iBook] with my new smarth-phone because of a "strange" implementation of the [en:SyncML] protocol on Sony Ericsson/UIQ 3.0 phones.

A lot of people has tried to figure out how to do it (Google for it), failing! But now [en:SonyEricsson] has finally released the relative plugins:

  • <a class="xlink" href="http://www.sonyericsson.com/spg.jsp?cc=global&lc=en&ver=4001&template=ps11311&zone=ps&lm=ps11&pid=10864&fid=43608&esi=true" target="blank">Sony Ericsson P1i
  • <a class="xlink" href="http://www.sonyericsson.com/spg.jsp?cc=global&lc=en&ver=4001&template=ps11311&zone=ps&lm=ps11&pid=10336&fid=43613&esi=true" target="blank">Sony Ericsson P990i
  • <a class="xlink" href="http://www.sonyericsson.com/spg.jsp?cc=global&lc=en&ver=4001&template=ps11311&zone=ps&lm=ps11&pid=10391&fid=43623&esi=true" target="_blank">Sony Ericsson W950i

Enjoy! ;-)

Update 15th August 2007: BTW, the plugins (I tested on only the one for W950i) doesn't synchronize Tasks (Todos). I really don't know why ( :?: ). But it's already a good lip ahead.
