Stephen Fry has opened a Blog

Sunday, 30 Sep 2007 — art curiosity personal uk english movies-tv

From [en:Stephen_Fry|Wikipedia]:

Stephen John Fry (born 24 August 1957) is an English comedian, writer, actor, novelist, filmmaker and television personality. The former comedy collaborator of [en:Hugh Laurie], he is also the host of the television panel game [en:QI].
I like this man. I discovered him thanks to Laurie, and reading and watching him I discovered that he is great on his own.

Fry and his FryPhone (is not my idea: is him)

And now he has a blog. Just 2 post, but both interesting a sharper than you can expect from a "Theater/Letterature Man". Particularly, I suggest the first one about SmartPhones, iPhone and (hypothetical) iPhone-Killers: Devices and Desires.

Best Wishes for Your new "experiment", Mr. Fry. ;)

P.S. If you want to understand how good is this man as actor, watch "[en:A Bit of Fry and Laurie]" (all the 4 seasons): I bought all the DVDs. As soon as I finish my third english-written book, Mortal Fear by Greg Iles, I'll buy (or I'll borrow from the Symbian Library) [en:The_Liar|The Liar by Fry]: then, I'll "evaluate" him also as writer.
