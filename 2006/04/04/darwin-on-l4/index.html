Darwin on L4

Tuesday, 04 Apr 2006 — apple macosx sw operating-systems it opensource curiosity english bsd projects

Operating Systems based on Micro-Kernel seems to return fashionable, and some experimental OS releases demonstrate it: the National ICT of Australia, infact, has released a Darwin porting for L4. This is for being able to study the behaviour of that class of o.s..

This porting include XNU, the hearth of MacOSX.

What's L4?

L4 is, collectively, a family of related computer programs. They are microkernels that are becoming well known in the computer industry for their excellent performance and small footprint...
