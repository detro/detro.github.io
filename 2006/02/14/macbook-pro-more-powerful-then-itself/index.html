MacBook Pro: more powerful then itself

14 Feb 2006

Singular but nice move from Apple: every booked (and now in distribution) MacBook Pro will be delivered with a SpeedBump Gift!

Core Duo 1,83GHz will be delivered with a Core Duo 2Ghz, the 1,67GHz with a 1,83GHz.

And, as it's not enough, a new Core Duo 2,16GHz is be avalaible as an order-option!!!

Source, Melablog.
