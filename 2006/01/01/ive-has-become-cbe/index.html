Ive has become CBE

01 Jan 2006

Commander of the British Empire.

Queen Elizabeth II has inserted Johnathan Ive in the New Year Honours list of Great Britain.

... He is widely seen as one of the key figures in Apple's resurrection. Mr Ive started working for Apple in 1992 but exerted a big influence on its products only in 1997 when Steve Jobs returned to the company he co-founded. Mr Ive's first design for Apple, the iMac, was hugely influential and has been followed by a series of other widely admired gadgets.

Source. On BBC News.
