Video of the Day

21 Aug 2006

KLOSS does not sell many things, but stomachs need to be filled, and the price of erasers keeps rising. After over two years in development, 'DeLighTable' is finally avaliable to buy as a commercial product. The real work obviously lies in the pressure sensitive surface, but now it also comes in an attractive casing. With a framework finished in polished stainless steel, DeLighTable has taken the guise of a slim coffe table only twenty five millimetres thick. To purchase, simply fill in a form through the 'contact us' section. The tables are made to order, and the typical lead time is four to six weeks.
