I have already highlighted in the past this project. Now I'm delighted to report that version 1.7 is out, and... it's a great one!!!

My favourite feature? Discover! As soon as I installed the addon in Firefox (very notable: it's already compatible with the brand new version 3 of my favourite browser), I spent something like 40 minutes just browsing the "movies/tv" section, full of very updated and interesting content... and presented in the most "sexy" way I have ever seen.

Again, a great piece of Software. Strongly recommended to give it a go!

PS Just to avoid to forget, Piclens works practically ANY browser ;) .
