Cleaning Apple "Classic" Keyboard

  • Sunday, 06 Jul 2008
  • old • curiosity • classic • personal • keyboard • it • geek • clean • english • technology • apple

You know, I’m picky when it’s about woman and technology (and food, but not as much as for the previous). Apple, a while ago now, get rid of the the normal/classical keyboard with big and deep keys (that every proper geek/software engineer loves)…

… to replace it with the crap, ultra slim, no-noisy, no-tasty, no-feedback alluminum+plastic keyboard.

A shame. A real shame.

So, what I did? I bought on eBay the old model (for £25.00). What’s the matter? It was dirty. At least for my standards :P . So, I found this post that explained how to remove the keys and clean everything properly.

So, now, you can enjoy with me my beautiful, ultra-clean, keyboard ;)
