Franz Kafka is an Idiot

Monday, 29 Sep 2008 — novel art franz-kafka personal disagree read book metamorphosis english

I just read "The Metamorphosis". And I liked it... until I reached the last page of it. Where I understood that Franz Kafka is an Idiot.

First Edition Cover

I like of course very much the quality and Franz is, out of any doubt, a great novelist... but I totally disagree with the message, the perspective and the conclusion of it.

P.S. I read it with the great Stanza for iPhone.
