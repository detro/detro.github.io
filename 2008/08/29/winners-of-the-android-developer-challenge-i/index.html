Winners of the Android Developer Challenge I

Friday, 29 Aug 2008 — announcement google mobile challenge it applications curiosity english android developer

Google finally announced the winners of the First Round of the Android Developer Challenges. There are 10 x $275,000 prizes and 10 x $100,000 prizes. $3,750,000 in prizes!!!. Not bad I must say.

There are some pretty cool apps, with a very clean and nice UI: demonstration of the power and flexibility of the Android Graphical Widgets Framework. Take a look at them in the official gallery.

Congratulations to the Winners!!!
