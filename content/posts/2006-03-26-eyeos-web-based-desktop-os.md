> Welcome to eyeOS, a web based desktop system. With eyeOS you can access your data and your applications anywhere, anytime. A virtual office in your hands, no need to install anything in the computer. Everything lives in the browser, for you and your work colleagues. eyeOS is open source and free software. [Set up freely an account on our servers](http://www.eyeos.info/) or, if you prefer it, [install it in your server](http://www.eyeos.org/Downloads). Make your life easier with the virtual word processor, calendar, file manager, messenger, browser and other applications. And if you want more applications, just visit the [eyeOS Application Database](http://www.eyeapps.org/)!
