The [Queen](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elizabeth_II_of_the_United_Kingdom) have started to make a series of [Podcast about her Speeches](http://www.royal.gov.uk/output/Page5323.asp). This service is offered by the [British Monarchy Media Center](http://www.royal.gov.uk/output/page3759.asp).

It's not so important, but this is a sing of the young of this Monarchy, with a big, rich and important story, but the ability to reorder and resync itself respect the changeing world.

Every English person does not see the Queen as a [Power](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Political_power), but as a Symbol. A [Symbol](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Symbol) of the [History](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/History), the [Tradition](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tradition), the [Importance](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Importance).

Probably, My [Country](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italy) is richer about Symbol and History, but it's (and I'm, because I'm a part of it) not so smarty.
