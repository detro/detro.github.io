It's going to be quite the challenge, and to make sure I'm ready for the big day, I'll be putting in some serious training hours 💪 over the coming weeks and months. I'm looking forward to getting out on the road and pushing my limits.

More importantly, **I'm riding _and_ fund raising** for a fantastic cause. I am raising funds for [Action Medical Research](https://action.org.uk/), an incredible charity that funds vital research to help sick and disabled babies, children, and young people. 

To keep myself accountable (and to prove I'm actually putting the miles in! 😎), I've set up a **[Just Giving campaign page](https://www.justgiving.com/fundraising/ivandemarino-69a29df97584da393b712902?utm_medium=FR&utm_source=CL&utm_campaign=020)** where you can track [my training progress](https://www.justgiving.com/fundraising/ivandemarino-69a29df97584da393b712902#timeline-section-marker).

If you have a bit to spare, it would be absolutely great if you could **contribute to this cause**. Every little bit really does help and goes toward making a tangible difference. If you can't donate right now, simply cheering me on, [sharing the link](https://www.justgiving.com/fundraising/ivandemarino-69a29df97584da393b712902?utm_medium=FR&utm_source=CL&utm_campaign=020), or offering some words of encouragement means just as much!

Or, even better, [join me in May](https://action.org.uk/events/cycling/garmin-ride-out)!

Wish me and my legs luck! 😮‍💨
