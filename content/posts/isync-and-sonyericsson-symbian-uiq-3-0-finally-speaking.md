![W950i](http://www.elite-electronix.com/images/2489-W950i.jpg)
Thanks God, now I can [en:iSync]!!!

[As someone probably remember](http://www.detronizator.org/2007/07/08/risposta-ad-ossblogit/), I switched from an [en:Nokia E70] to a [en:Sony Ericsson W950i].

In the beginning, it was not possible to synchronize my [en:iBook] with my new smarth-phone because of a "strange" implementation of the [en:SyncML] protocol on Sony Ericsson/UIQ 3.0 phones.

A lot of people has tried to figure out how to do it ([Google](http://www.google.com/) for it), failing! But now [en:Sony_Ericsson] has finally released the relative plugins:

   * [Sony Ericsson P1i](http://www.sonyericsson.com/spg.jsp?cc=global&lc=en&ver=4001&template=ps1_1_3_1_1&zone=ps&lm=ps1_1&pid=10864&fid=43608&esi=true)

   * [Sony Ericsson P990i](http://www.sonyericsson.com/spg.jsp?cc=global&lc=en&ver=4001&template=ps1_1_3_1_1&zone=ps&lm=ps1_1&pid=10336&fid=43613&esi=true)

   * [Sony Ericsson W950i](http://www.sonyericsson.com/spg.jsp?cc=global&lc=en&ver=4001&template=ps1_1_3_1_1&zone=ps&lm=ps1_1&pid=10391&fid=43623&esi=true)

Enjoy! ;-) 

<hr /><strong>Update 15th August 2007</strong>: BTW, the plugins (I tested on only the one for W950i) doesn't synchronize Tasks (Todos). I really don't know why ( :?: ). But it's already a good lip ahead.
