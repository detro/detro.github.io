Normally I don't spend time suggesting Add-ons/Plugins for [Firefox](http://www.mozilla.org/firefox/) or other stuff like that, but this is GREAT!

[Cooliris PicLens](http://www.piclens.com) ([here the Firefox Add-on Page](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/5579)), available on Windows & Mac; working with [en:Internet Explorer|IE], Firefox and [en:http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Safari_(web_browser)|Safari] (too!!!).

Just install it, then go on any [Flickr](http://www.flickr.com), [Picasa](http://picasa.google.com/), [Facebook](http://www.facebook.com/), wathever service that expose an [en:RSS] feed of pictures and... click on the PicLens icon (in Firefox will be in the upper right corner after the installation).

[Sweetheart](http://strangelelly.wordpress.com/), I'm sure you will love it ;)
