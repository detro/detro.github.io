Then, I received a fake-mail from [PayPal](http://www.paypal.co.uk/), pretending to be the receipt of the payment. I checked, also on the PayPal website to be sure.

So, I spent €28 as fees for the Auctions... for nothing. I asked to eBay to have my money back... but I think there is no hope.
