Soon you may be able to execute Gnome/Gtk+ based programs <del datetime="2006-02-01T10:26:09+00:00">NATIVELLY</del> NATIVELY on Quartz without the X11 Emulation.
[Someone (thanks to exist) is working on the porting](http://micke.hallendal.net/archives/2005/10/gtk-macosx.html).

It may be the <em>incipit</em> of a new era for Open Source software on MacOSX. Take a look at [this](http://www.gnomefiles.org/).

One of the most interesting characteristics of Gnome-oriented applications is the coherence between the [HIG (Human Interface Guidelines)](http://developer.gnome.org/projects/gup/hig/) of Gnome and MacOSX.

Source: [Melablog.it](http://www.melablog.it/post/1444/in-arrivo-gimp-os-x-nativo) and [StyleMac.com](http://www.stylemac.com/2006/01/28/native-gimp-read-no-x11-anymore-coming-to-mac-os-x/).
