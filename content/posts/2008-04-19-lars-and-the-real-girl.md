[![Lars and The Real Girl](http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/d/db/Lars_real_girl.jpg)](http://www.larsandtherealgirl-themovie.com/)
Yesterday, waiting for [Morfeus](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morpheus_%28mythology%29) ([the God](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morpheus_%28mythology%29), not [the Blue-Pill-Red-Pill Freak](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morpheus_%28The_Matrix%29)) to catch me and bring me to sleep. I had this movie in my beloved [iPhone](http://www.detronizator.org/tag/iphone/), "[Lars and the Real Girl](http://www.larsandtherealgirl-themovie.com/)".

It's pure gold. A movie about a guy, Lars ([Ryan Gosling](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0331516/)), who has a very strange mental disease, that needs a Girlfriend but is unable to catch-and-kiss the classical Next-Door-Girl ([Kelli Garner](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0307726/)) that loves him... also if she is aware of his sickness.

[More info and the full plot is on Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lars_and_the_Real_Girl) (for whom likes spoilering ;) ).

What I strongly suggest to follow is the recitation of what is not said: eyes, movements, expressions, breaths too. Everything is done in a perfect harmony. The perception is of a classical American town that is completely disconnected from the outside world, and mainly because of this is able to "help" Lars.

Best character? Dr. Dagmar ([Patricia Clarkson](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0165101/)). Her recitation is pure gold, a perfect not-yet-so-old woman who lost her husband and... now is just... lonely.
During an "interview" with Lars she speaks about her self:


>> [...]
> Sometimes I get so lonely I forget what day it is, and how to spell my name.
> [...]




I strongly suggest to give a try to this movie. It's slow and "delicate": give it the time to catch your interest, and pay attention to every whispering (in there is the pure gold of this movie).
