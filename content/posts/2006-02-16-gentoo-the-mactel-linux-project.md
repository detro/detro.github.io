I was one of the first Gentoo-on-iBook-7450 installer: I have an idea of the obstacles that Apple make (voluntarily?) to stop the "waste" of our hardware.

The installation of [Gentoo on Mactel](http://xbox-linux.org/mactel/index.php/Main_Page) is only a "time issue": [EFI](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Extensible_Firmware_Interface) was, for me, the biggest problem ([elilo](http://sourceforge.net/projects/elilo) rulez!). But I don't know the internal hardware of that new machine.

Anyway, Good Work guys.
