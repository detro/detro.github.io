By mistake I missed the first issue of <strong><em>"10 Politically Incorrect Poetry"</em></strong> (a semantic translation of <strong><em>"10 Poesie Incivili"</em></strong>) from [Andrea Camilleri](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andrea_Camilleri) ([Wiki in Italian](http://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andrea_Camilleri)).

A masterpiece. Published by [MicroMega](http://temi.repubblica.it/micromega-online/), the [first issue](http://temi.repubblica.it/micromega-online/camilleri-legge-camilleri-dieci-poesie-incivili-video/) contained 10 poetry, [the second](http://temi.repubblica.it/micromega-online/camilleri-legge-cinque-nuove-poesie-incivili-video/) 5 (and sounds like there will be others...).

They are all about [Berlusconi](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Silvio_Berlusconi), [Fini](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gianfranco_Fini), [Bossi](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Umberto_Bossi) and the "Gentle-Opposition" of the [Italian Democratic Party](http://static.wikipedia.org/new/wikipedia/en/articles/d/e/m/Democratic_Party_(Italy)_a617.html).
The only problem: they are in Italian, sorry! But we talk about a "Master", so a try it's worth it.
