Last 2/3 months I used a bit of my free time studying [Android](http://code.google.com/android/) the so called "Google OS", that is the result of the [Open Handset Alliance](http://www.openhandsetalliance.com/).

In this relatively short amount of time I had the possibility to collect information from the official sources, as well as from very interesting and active forums (like [this one](http://www.anddev.org/) and [this one](http://www.modmygphone.com/forums/)). I had also the possibility to meet other experts during the <strong>Android Code Day</strong> event ([here](http://blog.airsource.co.uk/index.php/2008/02/07/android-code-day/) a summary what we did there). It was a very good place to ask important business related questions: I should say that the Google Developer Advocate <strong>Jason Chen</strong> was very keen to answer my tricky questions ;-) .

Spending other words on this here is quite pointless: half of the web speaks about it (while the other half speaks about [iPhone](http://www.apple.com/iphone/) ;) ). But I would like to share part of the result of this study:

	* A Presentation: "Into the Android" [[PDF](http://downloads.detronizator.org/intotheandroid/Into the Android.pdf) | [HTML+Flash](http://downloads.detronizator.org/intotheandroid/Into the Android.html)]

	* Video #1: MWC - Android running on different ARM-based devices [[mp4](http://downloads.detronizator.org/intotheandroid/MWC - Android running on different ARM-based devices.mp4)]

	* Video #2: MWC - Android running on E2831 [[mp4](http://downloads.detronizator.org/intotheandroid/MWC - Android running on E2831.mp4)]

	* Source code of the Application I developed for study (actually, is just the [Tutorial](http://code.google.com/android/intro/tutorial.html) that comes with the [SDK](http://code.google.com/android/download.html) with much more internal documentation ;) ): [VSNotepad.tar.gz](http://downloads.detronizator.org/intotheandroid/VSNotepad.tar.gz).

The presentation explains different details of the Development process using the code of this application as an example.
