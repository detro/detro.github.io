[Callware VoiceOne](http://www.voiceone.it/index.php) is a web-app with an user-friendly look&feel about the management of [PBX](http://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/PBX) based on [Asterisk Realtime Architecture (ARA)](http://www.asterisk.org/) platform.
![VoiceOne](http://www.voiceone.it/admin//public/img/logo.gif)

With this software, it's possible to control the internal telephone line of our (:D) company.
It's possible to manage:

* Internal numbers

* [VoIP](http://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Voip) Providers

* Queue and multilevel permissions

* Automatic responser systems

The application is under the Open Source LGP license and require Linux.
Actually, [0.1 version is available](http://www.voiceone.it/changelog/).
