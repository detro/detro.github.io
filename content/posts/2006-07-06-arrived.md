The [ShipIt](https://shipit.ubuntu.com/) project by [Canonical](http://www.canonical.com/) (the Company behind Ubuntu Linux) was true: yeasterday a big package with 10 CD with [Ubuntu Linux 6.06](http://www.ubuntu.com/) [Dapper Drake](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DapperDrake) was arrived to me.

	

* 8 for x86

	* 1 for AMD64 and EM64T

	* 1 for G3, G4, G5

The package contains also 12 Ubuntu "linux for human beings" stickers.

Thanks to [Canonical](http://www.canonical.com/)!
