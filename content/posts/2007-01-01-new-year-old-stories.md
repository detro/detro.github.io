No, It's not a post on politics or demagogy: it's about foolishness!!!
[HD-DVD](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HD_DVD), one of the [2 "standards"](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blu-ray_Disc) of the future of Home-Movie (and Digital Video Storage in general), seems to be cracked... already.

An anonymous cracker, called <strong>muslix64</strong>, have published a post on [Doom9](http://forum.doom9.org/showthread.php?t=119871) about a very little Java program that is capable of Rip [AACS](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AACS)-Encrypted video from an HD-DVD.

More info on a page of Wikipedia dedicated to this program, [BackupHDDVD](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BackupHDDVD).
Source, [The Inquier IT](http://it.theinquirer.net/2006/12/decrittato_il_sistema_aacs.html).
