Microsoft, because of the STOP to development of [Windows Media Player for MacOSX](http://www.microsoft.com/mac/otherproducts/otherproducts.aspx?pid=windowsmedia), make a [page](http://www.microsoft.com/windows/windowsmedia/player/flip4mac.mspx) on our site where we can download a nice thing: a plugin for QuickTime aims to view Windows Media Files.

Seems to be a good product: I will update you soon.

The plugin is [Flip4Mac](http://www.flip4mac.com/wmv_upgrades.htm). The company behind this piece of code is Telestream, that [sell other plugins to import/edit WMF](http://www.flip4mac.com/wmv_upgrades.htm).
