Developers of [Firefox](http://www.mozilla.org/projects/firefox/), [Thunderbird](http://www.mozilla.org/projects/thunderbird/) and [Camino](http://www.caminobrowser.org/) are working on a [Universal Binary](http://www.apple.com/it/universal/) release: they have promised a official release in march.

![](http://www.melablog.it/uploads/MozillaUniversal.jpg)

By now, [here are sufficiently stable releases](http://dw.com.com/redir?destUrl=http%3A%2F%2Fwiki.mozilla.org%2FMac%3AIntel&siteId=3&oId=2100-1032-6027369&ontId=1023&lop=nl.ex). But, problems still remain: Java-plugin and Flash-plugin does not run, right now. On MacIntel (if you are so lucky ;) ) use [Firefox build for PPC](http://www.mozilla.com/) over [Rosetta](http://www.apple.com/it/rosetta/).

Source, [Melablog](http://www.melablog.it/post/1359/firefox-thunderbird-e-camino-per-mac-intel).
