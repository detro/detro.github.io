[Operating Systems](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Operating_system) based on [Micro-Kernel](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microkernel) seems to return fashionable, and some experimental OS releases demonstrate it: the [National ICT of Australia](http://www.ertos.nicta.com.au/software/darbat/), infact, has released a [Darwin](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Darwin_%28operating_system%29) porting for [L4](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/L4_microkernel_family). This is for being able to study the behaviour of that class of o.s..

This porting include [XNU](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xnu), the hearth of [MacOSX](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MacOSX).
