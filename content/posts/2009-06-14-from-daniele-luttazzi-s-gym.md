This morning, while I was reading the [Daniele Luttazzi's blog](http://www.danieleluttazzi.it/) and his series of post called <strong>"La Palestra"</strong> (<strong>"The Gym"</strong> in English), I decided that was worth it to translate some of the best I read.
"The Gym"? [Luttazzi](http://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daniele_Luttazzi) publishes satyric jokes on his blog, from a collection of ones that he receives from his fans.
But why? Because I think some of them are good even in English. Italian's latest events are being publicized on international newspapers lately. This makes it possible to get those jokes.

There you go!


![No Jokes Please](http://wrongcrowd.com/albums/misc/no_jokes_please.jpg)
