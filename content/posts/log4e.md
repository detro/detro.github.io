> [Log4E](http://log4e.jayefem.de/index.php/Main_Page) is an [Eclipse](http://www.eclipse.org/) [Plugin](http://eclipse-plugins.info/eclipse/plugin_details.jsp?id=611) which helps you to set up your logger easily in Java Projects. It assists you in several tasks: logger declaration, logger Insertions at certain method entries, substitution of System out's, modification of already existing logger statements. Log4E has active support for [Log4j](http://logging.apache.org/log4j/docs/index.html), [Commons Logging](http://jakarta.apache.org/commons/logging/) and [JDK 1.4 Logging](http://java.sun.com/j2se/1.4.2/docs/guide/util/logging/). By defining your own templates you might be able to adapt your own logger to the Plugin.



A very usefull plugin for every [Eclipse](http://www.eclipse.org/) Java Developer, that knows the importance of Logging... expecially in huge projects.
