![Google I/O 2009](http://code.google.com/events/io/images/io2009.png)Just a quick one.
First day of the [Google I/O 2009](http://code.google.com/events/io/) in [San Francisco (CA)](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/San_Francisco,_California).
Apart from a lot of informative and interesting topics and breakout sessions (I mainly focused on App Engine and Android), I came back at home full of:

* Stickers

* T-shirt

* [Google Chrome comic paperback version](http://www.google.com/googlebooks/chrome/)

* A brand new "Google I/O device", that is a black [HTC Magic](http://www.htc.com/www/product/magic/overview.html) sim and firmware unlocked for developers + Android Market with Paid Apps support + Amazon Mp3 store +...!!!. How much? For FREE, of course!!!

Thanks Google for all the amazing stuff, presentation, technologies, gifts... and food! ;)
