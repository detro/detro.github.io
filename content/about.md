## Who is "Zio"?

⚙️ I’ve spent over **20 years** in the engine room of software engineering,
and if there’s one thing two decades of shipping code has taught me,
it’s that the best architectures—and the best lives—are built
on **strong opinions, held loosely.**

🇮🇹 -> 🇬🇧 I’m a [Neapolitan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Naples)
that traded the Mediterranean sun 🥵
for the **UK tech scene back in 2007**.
Since then, I’ve been tolerating British weather 💂 with the same grit
I use to lead engineering teams, always aiming for that rare sweet spot ⚖️
between technical excellence and human pragmatism. Always sprinkled with
plenty of 👨 _dad jokes_!

In my world, some things are built to be refactored, while others are set in stone.
My code is flexible and my leadership style is adaptive,
but my **"al dente" is absolute** 🍝. I have a lifetime record of
**zero overcooked pasta**: being from **Napoli**, I’ve spent the last
**10+ years** in a relentless pursuit of the perfect Neapolitan pizza 🍕.
I’ve moved past the traditional and into the **"Contemporanea" style**:
even a classic recipe deserves a modern, airy upgrade.

💡 **That same spirit of modernization defines my career.**
In a software world where the only constant is change, I refuse to be legacy.
Whether it's mastering a new paradigm or refactoring my own leadership approach,
I have a restless desire to learn, grow, and reinvent myself.
Just as the *Contemporanea* style honours _"La Tradizione"_ while pushing
the _boundaries_ of what a _crust_ can be (visual pun intended 😜),
I believe in **respecting the fundamentals**
of engineering while **improving the execution** and not being
afraid of **reinventing**.

🚴 When I’m not at the keyboard or mentoring the next generation of Tech Leads,
I’m likely chasing elevation. I only fell into the **cycling** rabbit hole
three years ago. In that short window, I’ve already clocked
over **40 vertical kilometers** on two wheels. Life, it turns out,
is simply better at a 10% gradient: nothing beats the demons 👺 of stress, like
sweating profusely 😅 on a saddle.

👪 I’m an **atti-dude**, but I don't get away with much! While I’m busy
being the "Tech Lead" out in the world, it’s **my beautiful wife** 💍 who truly
keeps me and my attitude in check.
She’s the one who ensures my "strong opinions" don't turn into
"stubborn delusions"; and our **two incredible sons** provide and require
the high-intensity focus that keeps my life busy and grounded.

### Why "Zio Ivan"?

I’ve always liked the vibe of the "Zio" (Uncle), but it became official
in **2026** when [my little sister](https://www.linkedin.com/in/lorena-de-marino-a99b8379/)
made me a Zio for the first time 🤱.

I fell in love with how the expression **"Zio Ivan"** sounds: the person
you go to for the unfiltered truth, the mentor who has seen the fire and
lived to tell the tale, and the one who always knows how to fix the dough.

_I hope I will, one day, live up to this aspiration._
