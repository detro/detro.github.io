ecto: help me to Blog, please.

12 Jun 2005


ecto is a feature-rich desktop blogging client for MacOSX and Windows, supporting a wide range of weblog systems, such as Blogger, Blojsom, Drupal, MovableType, ...

ecto is the successor of the wildly popular Kung-Log, which has been in use by thousands of Mac users ...

With ecto you can write and manage entries for your weblog(s). The advantage over using your weblog's control panel is that you can compose entries offline and use the extra features ecto offers, such as spellcheck, creating links, attachments, and much more. ecto is designed to make blogging much more easier and yet give the users as much power as possible to manage their weblogs.



I'm trying this software about two hours and... I'm in love ;-) ecto (writed with little-case "e") use the WebService API exposed by various Blog-System to transform the Blogging in a very easy and fast game.
