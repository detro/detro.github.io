Manage ARA PBX easy

  • Friday, 23 Dec 2005
  • english • sw • it • opensource • linux • projects
  • 68 words

Callware VoiceOne is a web-app with an user-friendly look&feel about the management of PBX based on Asterisk Realtime Architecture (ARA) platform.

With this software, it’s possible to control the internal telephone line of our (:D) company. It’s possible to manage:

  • Internal numbers
  • VoIP Providers
  • Queue and multilevel permissions
  • Automatic responser systems

The application is under the Open Source LGP license and require Linux. Actually, 0.1 version is available.
